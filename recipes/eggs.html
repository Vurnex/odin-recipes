Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Scrambled Eggs Done Right

The right way to scramble eggs. There is more to just mixing eggs and cooking! This will make a believer out of you.

Preparation is 2 minutes. Cooking time 4 minutes. A total of 6 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of mayonnaise or salad dressing
  • 1 teaspoon of water (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon of margarine or butter
  • 1 pinch of salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. In a cup or small bowl, whisk together the eggs, mayonnaise and water using a fork.
  2. Melt margarine in a skillet over low heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs, and stir constantly as they cook.
  4. Remove the eggs to a plate when they are set, but still moist.
  5. Do not over cook. Never add salt or pepper until eggs are on plate, but these are also good without.
